papio provider access

Grant access per source. Access is only used to complete one explicitly requested download after you have signed in yourself. Login, MFA, and CAPTCHAs always stay with you. Revoking returns the source to assisted behaviour immediately.

    Library resolver access

    Firefox asks you to grant this access at runtime; Chrome grants it when the extension is installed. papio uses it to resolve your library's links when completing an explicitly requested download.

      Publisher terms & conditions

      Some publishers require accepting their terms & conditions before a download. When enabled, papio accepts these automatically on your behalf. This is a legal agreement to each publisher's terms; leave it off to accept them yourself in the browser.

      Background work window

      papio does its browsing in one dedicated minimized window instead of your tab strip, and only brings a tab forward when you are needed (for example institutional sign-in). Turn this off to open papio tabs in your current window instead.
