Abstract

  • The article focuses on how Americans define and pursue happiness 250 years after it became a founding ideal, showing that fulfillment comes through personal goals, meaningful experiences, and connections with others. Topics include diverse paths to happiness such as career ambitions and creative passions, community and family relationships, and the search for purpose, joy, and personal growth.
